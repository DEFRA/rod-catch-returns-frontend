Privacy Notice: How we use your personal information

We are the Environment Agency and we run the rod catch returns service. We are the data controller for this service. A data controller determines how and why personal data (personal information) is processed.

Our personal information charter explains your rights and how we deal with your personal information. You can access the charter using the link or go to gov.uk and search 'Environment Agency personal information charter'.

The personal data we need

  • Full name
  • Contact details
  • Details of your fishing activity

We are allowed to process your personal data because we have official authority as the environmental regulator, and set out in the National Fisheries Byelaws The lawful basis for processing your personal data is to perform a task in the public interest that is set out in law.

What we do with your personal data

We need this data of your fishing activity in order to manage salmon and sea trout stocks in a sustainable way. If you don't provide all of this information then we can't do this. We use your personal data to better understand the wellbeing of salmon and sea trout.

We do not use your personal data to make an automated decision or for automated profiling.

We will not share or disclose your personal data to any party outside the Environment Agency without your explicit consent, unless we are lawfully able to do so.

How long we keep your personal data

We will keep your personal data for 3 years.

Where your personal data is processed and stored

We store and process your personal data on our servers within the European Economic Area (EEA). We will not transfer your personal data outside the EEA.

We welcome your views on this service. We invite feedback through an online survey tool provided by SmartSurvey Ltd. We’ll use what you tell us to improve this and related services.
